The Best Roasted Carrots By Wes

1. Set oven to 425C.
2. Line roasting pan with parchment paper.
3. Peel 5lbs of carrots.
4. Top and tail them.
5. Cut into long strips.
6. Lay onto the roasting pan.
7. Drizzle with olive oil.
8. Sprinkle with your choice of herbs and spices.
9. Season with salt and pepper.
10. Cover with tin foil.
11. Bake for 30 minutes or until tender.
12. Remove foil and grill on high for 5 minutes or until crispy.
13. Let cool, slightly.
14. Enjoy!
